Executive Insights

Summary of data findings.

Referral Dominates Volume

Referral represents the primary conversion engine, contributing 12.1K orders (>50% share of total) with a strong 12.19% CVR.

Organic Search Efficiency

Organic Search drives the highest overall session traffic volume (48.67% share), contributing 4,045 orders with a 1.58% CVR.

Paid/Social Budget Lag

Paid Search (3.62% CVR) and Social (0.42% CVR) account for less than 3% of orders. Recommend reviewing social marketing spends.

India Funnel Leakage

India is the 2nd largest regional traffic source (25.3K sessions) but records only 22 orders (0.09% CVR), pointing to extreme local checkout barriers.
